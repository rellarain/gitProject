Aesthetics

The aesthetic self is a dimension of personality that specifically relates to artistic experiences. Some of the functions within this self include:

  • Creativity
  • Creativity is defined by your ability to manipulate, alter, and create ideas.
  • Expression
  • Expression is defined by the formats of artistic expression you choose to practice.
  • Appreciation
  • Appreciation is defined by the elements that increase you enjoyment of a piece of art.
  • Interpretation
  • Interpretation is defined by the sources of information you look for when finding meaning in a piece of art.
  • Inspiration
  • Inspiration is defined by the vast kinds of artwork from around the world that inspire you.
  • Style
  • Style is defined by how you assemble the art of others to create a cohesive statement representing yourself.

Unity

The social self is a dimension of personality that specifically relates to social experiences. Some of the functions within this self include:

  • Self-Esteem
  • Self-Esteem is defined by your perceptions, feelings, and beliefs about yourself.
  • Interactivity
  • Interactivity is defined by your social preferences regarding who interacts with you and how.
  • Other-Esteem
  • Other-Esteem is defined by your perceptions, feelings, and beliefs about others.
  • Identity
  • Identity is defined by the social groups and related identifiers that connect to you.
  • Bonding
  • Bonding is defined by how you connect and form relationships with others.
  • Reception
  • Reception is defined by others' perceptions, feelings, and beliefs about you.

Resilience

The thriving self is a dimension of personality that specifically relates to the health and quality of life of an individual. Some of the functions within this self include:

  • Wellness
  • Wellness is defined by both your needs and how satisfied they are.
  • Adjustment
  • Adjustment is defined by how you cope with change in your life.
  • Beliefs
  • Beliefs are the semi-rigid systems of truths that you hold about reality.
  • Experiences
  • Expectations are defined by situations that have occurred in your life and how much they have effected you.
  • Health
  • Health is defined by the assessed quality of functioning by your body.
  • Expectations
  • Expectations are defined by an individual's outlook on the future.

Aptitude

The skilled self is a dimension of personality that specifically relates to the skills and abilities of an individual. Some of the functions within this self include:

  • Intelligence
  • Strengths
  • Drives
  • Literacy
  • Learning
